Gesture Training Deck

HOW TO PLAY

Use your hands inside the webcam frame to steer, rotate, drop, pause, and start the game.

Start

Clap Hands

Clap both hands together when the board says start to begin the round.

Move

Palm Left / Right

Move your open hand left or right in the camera frame to slide the piece.

Rotate CW

Thumb + Index

Pinch your thumb and index finger to rotate the piece clockwise.

Rotate CCW

Thumb + Middle

Pinch your thumb and middle finger to rotate the piece counterclockwise.

Soft Drop

Wave Down

Push a flat hand downward to make the current piece fall faster.

Pause

Hold V Sign

Hold a V sign for a moment to pause or resume the game.
